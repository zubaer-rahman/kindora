# Step 4: Guide for Organizations

This section is for **Organizations**. If you are looking for helpers, mentors, or volunteers, this guide will show you how to find them.

---

## 1. Organizing Your Profile

First, tell volunteers who you are.

**Step 1:** Go to your **Profile** page.
**Step 2:** Add your **Organization Name** and **Logo**.
**Step 3:** Write a description. What is your mission? Why should people help you?
**Step 4:** Add contact details and your website if you have one.

![Screenshot: Organization Profile Setup]

---

## 2. Creating an Opportunity (Posting a Job)

This is how you get help.

**Step 1:** On your Dashboard, find the button **"Create Opportunity"** or go to **Manage Opportunities** > **Create**.

**Step 2:** **Form Step 1: The Basics**
*   **Title**: Give it a catchy name (e.g., "Beach Cleanup Hero").
*   **Description**: Explain clearly what the volunteer needs to do.
*   **Category**: Pick a category (e.g., Environment).

![Screenshot: Create Opportunity Form - Step 1]

**Step 3:** **Form Step 2: Logistics**
*   **Date & Time**: When is it happening?
*   **Location**: Where should they go? Or check "Remote" if it is online.
*   **Capacity**: How many people do you need? (e.g., 5 volunteers).

![Screenshot: Create Opportunity Form - Step 2 (Logistics)]

**Step 4:** **Form Step 3: Requirements**
*   Add specific skills required (e.g., "Driving License").

**Step 5:** **Review & Publish**. Once you click Publish, it is live on the website for everyone to see!

---

## 3. Managing Applicants

People have applied! Now what?

**Step 1:** Go to **Manage Opportunities** in your sidebar.

**Step 2:** You will see a list of your posted jobs. Click on the one you want to check.

**Step 3:** Look for the **"Applicants"** tab or section. You will see a list of people.
*   **Click their name** to see their profile (skills, bio, past reviews).

![Screenshot: List of applicants for a specific opportunity]

**Step 4:** **Accept or Decline**.
*   **Accept**: They get a notification and are added to your team.
*   **Decline**: They are notified they were not chosen this time.

---

## 4. Editing or Deleting

Mistake in your post? No problem.

**Step 1:** In **Manage Opportunities**, find the card.
**Step 2:** Click the **Edit** (pencil icon). Make your changes and save.
**Step 3:** To remove it completely, click **Delete** (trash icon). *Be careful, this disappears forever!*

![Screenshot: Edit and Delete buttons on an opportunity card]
