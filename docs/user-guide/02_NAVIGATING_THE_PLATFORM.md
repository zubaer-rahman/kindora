# Step 2: Navigating the Platform

Congratulations on logging in! explicit Now let's look around. The Kindora interface is designed to be simple and easy to use.

---

## 1. The Top Navigation Bar

At the very top of every page, you will see a colored bar. This is your main way to move around.

*   **Logo (Top Left)**: Clicking the "Kindora" logo will always take you back to your **Home Dashboard**.
*   **Menu Items (Center)**:
    *   **Find Opportunities**: Browse all available listings.
    *   **Find Volunteers**: (If visible) Browse profiles of people ready to help.
    *   **About Us**: Learn more about the platform.
*   **Profile Menu (Top Right)**: You will see your profile picture or a small circle with your initials. Clicking this opens a dropdown menu.

![Screenshot: Top Navigation Bar with labels pointing to each section]

---

## 2. The Profile Dropdown

Clicking your picture in the top right corner shows these options:

*   **Profile**: View and edit your public profile.
*   **Dashboard**: Go to your main control center.
*   **Settings**: Change your password or account preferences.
*   **Logout**: Sign out of your account securely.

![Screenshot: Profile Dropdown menu open]

---

## 3. The Dashboard (Your Home)

When you log in, you land on the **Dashboard**. Think of this as your desk.

### For Volunteers
You will see:
*   **My Stats**: How many jobs you applied for, how many hours you worked.
*   **Recent Activity**: Updates on your applications.
*   **Recommended for You**: A list of opportunities that match your skills.

![Screenshot: Volunteer Dashboard view]

### For Organizations
You will see:
*   **Active Listings**: The jobs you currently have posted.
*   **Pending Applicants**: Number of people waiting for your approval.
*   **Upcoming Events**: Reminders for jobs starting soon.

![Screenshot: Organization Dashboard view]

---

## 4. The Sidebar (Left Menu)

On your Dashboard page, there is a menu on the left side.

*   **Overview**: Takes you back to the main dashboard summary.
*   **Messages**: Opens your chat inbox.
*   **My Opportunities / Manage Opportunities**: Where you see your lists of jobs.
*   **Profile**: shortcut to edit your details.

![Screenshot: Left Sidebar navigation menu]
