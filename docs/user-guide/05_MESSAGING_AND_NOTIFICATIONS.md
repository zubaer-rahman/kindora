# Step 5: Messaging & Notifications

Communication is key! Kindora has a built-in chat system so you don't need to share your personal phone number immediately.

---

## 1. Using the Chat

You can chat with people you are working with.

**Step 1:** Click the **Messages icon** (usually a speech bubble) in the sidebar or top bar.

**Step 2:** **Your Inbox**. On the left, you see a list of people you have chatted with.
*   **Unread Messages**: These will have a bold name or a notification dot.

![Screenshot: Main Messaging Inbox view]

**Step 3:** **Sending a Message**.
*   Click a name to open the chat.
*   Type in the box at the bottom.
*   Click the **Paper Plane icon** or press Enter to send.

**Step 4:** **Starting a New Chat**. Usually, you start a chat from a profile. Go to a user's profile and look for a **"Message"** button.

---

## 2. Notifications

How do you know if something happened?

**Step 1:** Look for the **Bell Icon** in the top right corner of the screen.

**Step 2:** If it has a **Red Dot**, you have new news!

**Step 3:** Click the Bell. A list will drop down showing:
*   "You have been accepted for..."
*   "New applicant for your opportunity..."
*   "New message from..."

![Screenshot: Notification dropdown open with examples]

---

## 3. Email Alerts

Important updates (like password resets or major acceptances) will also be sent to the email address you used to register. Check your email (and spam folder!) regularly so you don't miss anything.
