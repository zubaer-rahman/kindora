# Step 3: Guide for Volunteers

This section is for **Volunteers**. If you want to help out, join unique projects, or offer your skills, this guide is for you.

---

## 1. Setting Up Your Profile

Before you apply, you need to look good! Organizations are more likely to pick you if your profile is complete.

**Step 1:** Click on **Profile** in the sidebar or top menu.

**Step 2:** **Upload a Picture**. Click the camera icon to upload a professional photo of yourself.

**Step 3:** **Fill in the Basics**.
*   **Bio**: Write a short paragraph about yourself. Who are you? What do you like to do?
*   **Skills**: Select skills you have (e.g., Teaching, Coding, Driving, First Aid). *This helps us match you with the right jobs!*
*   **Interests**: Pick topics you care about (e.g., Environment, Education, Animals).

**Step 4:** Click **Save Changes**.

![Screenshot: Volunteer Profile Edit Page]

---

## 2. Finding Opportunities

Now, let's find some work!

**Step 1:** Click **"Find Opportunities"** in the top navigation bar.

**Step 2:** You will see a list of cards. Each card is a job.

**Step 3:** **Use Filters** (on the left or top).
*   **Search Bar**: Type keywords like "Music" or "Beach clean".
*   **Location**: Filter by where you live.
*   **Type**: Choose between "In-Person" or "Remote" (Work from home).

![Screenshot: Find Opportunities page showing filters and job cards]

**Step 4:** **View Details**. Click on any card that looks interesting. You will see:
*   The description of the work.
*   The date and time.
*   Where it is located.

---

## 3. Applying for an Opportunity

Found one you like? Let's apply!

**Step 1:** On the opportunity detail page, click the big **"Apply Now"** button.

![Screenshot: Opportunity Detail page with Apply button]

**Step 2:** A popup might appear asking for a short message. Write why you are a good fit (e.g., "I have 2 years of experience in this!").

**Step 3:** Click **Send Application**.

**Step 4:** **Wait for a response.** The organization will review your profile. You will get a notification if they accept or decline you.

---

## 4. Checking Your Application Status

Where did your application go?

**Step 1:** Go to your **Dashboard**.
**Step 2:** Click **My Opportunities** in the sidebar.
**Step 3:** Look at the tabs:
*   **Applied**: You are waiting for an answer.
*   **Ongoing**: You were accepted! Get ready to work.
*   **Completed**: Jobs you have finished.

![Screenshot: My Opportunities page with status tabs]
