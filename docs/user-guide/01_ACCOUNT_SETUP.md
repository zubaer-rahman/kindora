# Step 1: Account Setup & Login

This section covers the basics of getting into the Kindora platform. Follow these steps to create your account and sign in securely.

---

## 1. Creating a New Account (Sign Up)

If you have never used Kindora before, you need to sign up.

**Step 1:** Open the website. On the home page, look for the button labeled **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"** (usually at the top right corner).

![Screenshot: Homepage with Sign Up button highlighted]

**Step 2:** Click the button. You will be taken to the registration page.

**Step 3:** **Select Your Role**. This is very important!
*   **Volunteer**: Choose this if you are a person looking to apply for jobs or tasks.
*   **Organization**: Choose this if you are a company, non-profit, or group looking for people to help you.

![Screenshot: Registration page showing the two role options]

**Step 4:** Fill in your details.
*   **Name**: Your full name or organization name.
*   **Email**: A valid email address (we might send a confirmation link!).
*   **Password**: Choose a strong password that you can remember.

**Step 5:** Click the **"Create Account"** or **"Sign Up"** button at the bottom of the form.

> **Success!** You should now be redirected to the Login page or directly to your new Dashboard.

---

## 2. Logging In

Once you have an account, you can log in anytime.

**Step 1:** Click the **"Login"** button at the top right of the screen.

![Screenshot: Login button on the homepage]

**Step 2:** Enter your **Email Address** and **Password** in the boxes provided.

**Step 3:** (Optional) Check the box that says **"Remember me"** if you are on your own private computer. This keeps you logged in so you don't have to type your password every time.

**Step 4:** Click **"Sign In"**.

![Screenshot: Login form filled out]

---

## 3. Forgot Password?

If you can't remember your password, don't worry.

**Step 1:** On the Login page, click the link that says **"Forgot Password?"**.

![Screenshot: Forgot Password link highlighted on login page]

**Step 2:** Enter the email address you used to sign up.

**Step 3:** Check your email inbox. We will send you a link to reset your password. Click that link and create a new password.
